Who are we?

ASJA-MEXT Scholarship Recipients
アスジャ国費留学生

We are Japanese government (Monbukagakusho: MEXT) scholarship recipients from ten ASEAN countries and other countries including China, Egypt, etc..

Graduates From Japanese Universities
日本の大学・大学院の卒業生

Since 2000, there are more than 220 ASJA students who have graduated from Japanese universities.

A Global Network
グローバルネットワーク

ASJA graduates are now living and pursuing their career in Japan and in many different parts of the world.

Our mission is to create a sustainable, warm, and supportive ASJA community.
